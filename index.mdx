## What is AQL?

AQL is an AI platform designed specifically for Arabic language processing. We understand that Arabic presents unique challenges in AI applications—from its complex grammar to its rich variety of dialects. Our platform addresses these challenges head-on.

## What we do

We build AI solutions that work naturally with Arabic text, speech, and documents. Our technology handles everything from casual conversations in regional dialects to formal business communications.

Our approach combines three core capabilities:

- **Language understanding** that grasps context and nuance in Arabic
- **Knowledge systems** that learn from your specific content and data  
- **Practical tools** that developers and businesses can use immediately

## What we offer

### AQL GenAI
Our large language model trained specifically for Arabic. It understands various dialects and cultural contexts, making it more accurate for Arabic applications than general-purpose models.

### AI Knowledge
A system that learns from your documents and websites. Upload your content, and it becomes part of an intelligent knowledge base that can answer questions based on your specific information.

### AI Knowledge Assistant  
A conversational assistant that uses your knowledge base to provide accurate, context-aware responses. It's like having an expert who knows your content inside and out.

### APIs
Developer tools for integrating our capabilities into your applications:
- Chat completions and streaming
- Document-based question answering
- Speech-to-text transcription
- Text-to-speech synthesis

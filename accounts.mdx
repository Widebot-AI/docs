## Sign in / Sign up
- **Facebook** or **Google** SSO.
- **Email + Phone**: enter your email & phone, then verify if required.

## Profile basics
- Add your **first name** and **last name** from your account menu.
- **Change your password** for account security.
- **Generate a new API key** for accessing AQL services.

## Access control
- Your visible **Workspaces** depend on invitations and roles.
- For team access, see **Workspace Settings → Team**.
